Project Details

Index of Multiple Deprivation

Introduction

This project involved analyzing IMD data to gain socioeconomic insights and identify disadvantaged areas across local districts to guide decision-making.

Data Overview ðŸ“ˆ

The IMD datasets provided granular views into factors like income, employment, education, health, crime etc. at the neighborhood level based on official statistics. This enabled a multidimensional perspective.

Analysis Approach

Visualizations in Power BI focused on geographic distribution, decile-based segmentation and comparisons across factors and districts. This exposed priority areas. Additional data cleaning and shaping ensured reliability.

Key Insights

Signficant variability observed in metrics like income, employment, barriers to services across neighborhoods and districts. Certain regions demonstrated higher overall deprivation

Recommendations

Develop targeted interventions for high deprivation neighborhoods within districts, focused on tailoring programs as per primary drivers like income, skills etc. Channelize funding and resources accordingly.
